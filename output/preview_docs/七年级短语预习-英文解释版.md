# Grade 7 Phrase Preview Notes - Simple English Version

For students moving from Grade 6 to junior middle school. Read the meaning first, then read the example aloud.

## How to Study

1. Read five to ten phrases each day.
2. Cover the meaning and try to explain the phrase in your own words.
3. Make one new sentence about your school life.
4. Review old phrases before learning new ones.
